My Favorite Movies and Series

After getting all the work done, some snacks and watching a good movie or series is a perfect activity. As you can see I'm actually a series person more than movie.

Here is the list of some of my favorite movies and series.

  1. Friends



    This series, which tells about the years of friendship of 6 friends living in Manhattan, is one of my favorite sitcoms.

    I don't remember how many times I watched it, but it's still a series that I watch when I want to feel good.

  2. Anne with an E



    Based on "Anne of Green Gables.", a 13-year-old orphan girl searches for family, love and acceptance throughout her life in the orphanage.

    I had a small smile on my face when I finished this series that I saw and watched by chance on Netflix. I was very surprised that this series, which also deals with many social problems, lasted for 2 seasons.

  3. Harry Potter



    Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling. The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

    The world is divided into Lord of the Rings and Harry Potter lovers. I'm probably in the team Harry Potter. My favorite movie of this series that I watched for the first time in the cinema is "The Goblet of Fire".

  4. Modern Family



    Three modern-day families from California try to deal with their kids, quirky spouses and jobs in their own unique ways, often falling into hilarious situations.

    Modern Family is another sitcom I love, with each character unique and the funniest way to tell about still being a family in any situation.
